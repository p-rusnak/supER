## Usage

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Use the **Ratings** tab to add and rate escape rooms
3. Use the **Trip Planner** tab to plan your escape room adventure

### Adding an Escape Room
1. Go to the Ratings tab
2. Fill in the room name, location, and difficulty (1-10)
3. Click "Add Room"

### Rating a Room
1. Find the room in the list
2. Click on the stars to rate it (1-5 stars)
3. Your rating is saved automatically

### Planning a Trip
1. In the Ratings tab, click "Add to Trip" for rooms you want to visit
2. Switch to the Trip Planner tab
3. Set your trip duration and starting location
4. Click "Generate Trip Plan" to see your optimized itinerary
